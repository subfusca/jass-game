Game Rules

Setup

  • Each player draws 2 Patient Cards and chooses 1
  • Each player draws 3 Procedure Cards
  • Each player draws 4 Pharmacology Cards
  • Each player draws 1 Easy and 1 Challenging Modifier Card

Turn Phases

  1. Draw: Draw 1 Procedure and 1 Pharmacology Card
  2. Action: Play Procedure Cards with required Pharmacology Cards
  3. Modifiers: Optionally play Easy/Challenging Cards
  4. Replenish: Draw new Modifier Card if you played one

Winning

Complete all Procedure Cards and Pharmacology Requirements on your Patient Card. First player to complete 2 cases wins!
